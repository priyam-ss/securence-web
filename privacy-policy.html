Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This page outlines how SECURENCE handles, stores, and protects your personal data.

01

Introduction

SECURENCE respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you may share while interacting with this platform. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, handled, and safeguarded when you visit the SECURENCE website or engage with its features. As an independent project currently under development, SECURENCE aims to maintain transparency and responsible data practices. By using this platform, you acknowledge and agree to the terms described in this policy.

02

Information We Collect

SECURENCE may collect limited personal information that you voluntarily provide through forms available on the platform. This may include your name, email address, city, role, and any message or feedback you choose to submit. In addition, basic technical information such as browser type, device type, and IP-related data may be captured automatically through standard web server processes. The platform does not intentionally collect sensitive personal data unless explicitly provided by the user. In certain onboarding interactions, additional information such as phone number, physical address, profession, system-generated timestamps, browser/device information, and submission identifiers may also be collected as part of system functionality.

03

How We Collect It

Information is collected primarily through direct user interaction with the website. This includes submitting forms such as support requests, mission participation forms, or team applications. Data may also be collected automatically through standard website functionality when you browse or interact with pages. The platform does not use complex tracking mechanisms, hidden data collection techniques, or invasive monitoring systems.

04

Why We Collect It

The information collected is used solely to support the functioning and improvement of the SECURENCE project. This includes responding to user messages, reviewing applications, communicating updates, understanding user interest, and improving the overall experience of the platform. Data is not collected for profiling, advertising, or unrelated commercial purposes.

05

Data Storage & Security

SECURENCE uses a combination of cloud-based systems and server-side processing to handle user data. Certain information, specifically data submitted through onboarding systems, may be stored in a secure cloud database environment for operational and development purposes. Other form submissions may be processed and transmitted through server-side functions and delivered via secure email services. Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, as the platform is under active development, absolute security cannot be guaranteed. Users are advised to avoid submitting highly sensitive personal information.

06

Data Sharing

SECURENCE does not sell, rent, or trade user data under any circumstances. Information may only be shared with essential service providers required for platform functionality, such as email delivery systems, and only to the extent necessary for operation. Data may also be disclosed if required by applicable law, legal processes, or lawful authority requests.

07

Third-Party Services

SECURENCE uses third-party services to process and transmit form data. This includes hosting infrastructure and email delivery services. For example, form submissions may be processed through serverless functions and sent via email APIs. These services operate under their own privacy policies, and SECURENCE does not control their internal data handling practices. By using the platform, you acknowledge that such third-party services may be involved in processing your information.

07

User Rights

Users have the right to request access to the information they have provided and may request correction or deletion of their data where applicable. Since SECURENCE does not maintain a persistent database, such requests will be handled to the best extent possible based on available records. Users may contact the project directly to make such requests.

08

Cookies / Tracking

SECURENCE does not currently use cookies, tracking pixels, or behavioral analytics systems for monitoring user activity. Basic server-level logs may exist as part of hosting infrastructure to ensure security and performance stability. These logs are not used for profiling or user tracking purposes.

10

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in system functionality, legal requirements, or project development. Users are encouraged to review this page regularly. Continued use of the platform after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
